Who am I?

Discovering the person behind the professional journey is essential to grasping my deep-seated fascination with Amazon Web Services. I invite you to explore, firsthand, the experiences and motivations that fuel my passion for cloud innovation within the dynamic realm of AWS.

High School Beginnings: Web and UX Design

In the formative years of high school, I delved into the world of web and UX design, cultivating a passion that would shape my professional trajectory. During my senior year, I took on the exciting challenge of enhancing my school's digital presence by working on its website—a pivotal experience that laid the foundation for my journey into the realms of design and technology.

Navigating the Tech Landscape and UX Design Journey

Embarking on a journey that began with web and UX design, I seamlessly blended creativity with cutting-edge technology. This transformative phase, coupled with my work on my school's website, fueled my transition into the dynamic realm of cloud engineering. With 4 years of experience navigating the ever-evolving tech landscape, I specialized in UX design and obtained certification from Coursera, further enriching my skill set.

AWS Journey: Embracing the Power of AWS

Driven by a desire to explore unparalleled possibilities, I ventured into the world of Amazon Web Services (AWS). AWS became my platform of choice, offering not just tools and services, but a transformative ecosystem that aligns with my vision of pushing technological boundaries.

Per Scholas AWS re/Start Program | October 2023 - February 2023

Participating in the Per Scholas AWS re/Start Program from October 2023 to February 2023, I delved into the development of foundational cloud skills and gained hands-on experience with diverse AWS services. Applying this knowledge to real-world projects, I showcased problem-solving abilities and collaborative teamwork. Committed to comprehensive growth, I proactively pursued professional development in soft skills like communication, time management, and adaptability—essential traits for success in the dynamic IT environment.

Multilingual Flair: Bridging Cultures through Code

Beyond the lines of code, my ability to communicate in Haitian Creole, Spanish, and French brings a unique dimension to my professional identity. In a globalized tech landscape, being multilingual has proven invaluable, allowing me to bridge cultural gaps, empathize with team members and foster seamless collaboration.

Hobbies: Beyond the Code

When I'm not immersed in the world of technology, you'll often find me engaged in the harmonies of a piano, reveling in the power of music, or enjoying the thrill of a soccer match—either on the field or as an avid spectator. Balancing the rhythm of code with the symphony of life, I also cherish moments spent with friends, creating a harmonious blend of passion and connection.
